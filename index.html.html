Beyond big cites :tribal population

.Kamal khan Gakkhar, of Gakkhar tribe was a noble ( mansabdar) by emperor Akbar .
.In Multan and Sind , the langahs and Arghuns dominated extensive regions before they were by the Mughals.
.In the western Himalaya lived the shepherd tribe of GaddisThe distant north-eastern part of the subcontinent too was entirely dominated by tribes- the nagas, the ahom, etc.
.The Maharashtra highlands and Karnataka were home to kolis [ also in Gujarat], berads ,etc.
.South got koragas, vetars,and maravars,etc.

GOND TRIBES

.They practiced shifting cultivation.
.The Akbar Nama, a history of Akbar reign , mentions the Gond kingdom of Garha Katanga that had 70,000 villages.
.The emergence of large states changed the nature of gond society.
.Certain gond chiefs now wished to be recognized as Rajputs.

AHOM TRIBES

.During the 16th century, they annexed the kingdom of the chhutiyas(1523) and of Koch-Hajo (1581) and subjugated many other tribes.
.They know to use firearms as early as the 1530s.
.In 1662, the Mughals under mir jumla attacked on Ahom kingdom and defeated.
.The Ahom state depend upon forced labour. Those forced to work for the state were called paiks.
.By the 1660s they could even make high quality gunpowder and cannons.
.By the 17th century, the administration became quite and centralized. The first language of Ahoms was Assamese.
